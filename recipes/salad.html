Easy Coleslaw Dressing

This coleslaw dressing is easy to make and tastes great! It's perfect for a quick side dish or as a topping for sandwiches.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons white vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon celery seed (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, white vinegar, sugar, salt, black pepper, and celery seed (if using) until smooth and well combined.
  2. Taste the dressing and adjust the seasoning as needed. You can add more sugar for sweetness or more vinegar for tanginess.
  3. Pour the dressing over your favorite coleslaw mix (shredded cabbage and carrots) and toss to coat evenly.
  4. Refrigerate the coleslaw for at least 30 minutes before serving to allow the flavors to meld together.
  5. Serve chilled as a side dish or topping for sandwiches. Enjoy!
