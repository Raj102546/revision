Three Bean Potluck Casserole

This three bean casserole is a great side dish for potlucks and family gatherings. It's easy to make and packed with flavor!

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 (16 ounce) can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 (16 ounce) can pinto beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 1 cup chopped green bell pepper
  • 1 cup chopped celery
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the kidney beans, black beans, pinto beans, onion, green bell pepper, celery, and garlic. Stir in the diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, ketchup, brown sugar, Worcestershire sauce, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper. Mix well to combine.
  3. Transfer the bean mixture to a 9x13 inch baking dish. Cover with aluminum foil.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes. Remove the foil and sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese on top, if using. Bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  5. Remove from oven and let cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!
