Triple Seared Whiskey Burger

This burger is seared three times to create a crispy crust and juicy interior. The whiskey adds a unique flavor that pairs perfectly with the beef.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef (80% lean)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/4 cup whiskey
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • 4 hamburger buns, toasted
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and condiments of choice

Instructions

  1. Preheat a cast-iron skillet over medium-high heat. Divide the ground beef into four equal portions and shape each portion into a patty. Season both sides of each patty with salt and black pepper.
  2. Add the olive oil to the hot skillet. Place the patties in the skillet and sear for 2-3 minutes on each side until a crust forms. Remove the patties from the skillet and set aside.
  3. Add the whiskey to the skillet, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom of the pan. Let it cook for about 1 minute to reduce slightly.
  4. Return the patties to the skillet and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness. During the last minute of cooking, place a slice of cheddar cheese on each patty to melt.
  5. Remove the patties from the skillet and let them rest for a few minutes. Assemble the burgers by placing each patty on a toasted bun and adding lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and your favorite condiments.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy your Triple Seared Whiskey Burger!
